Lost in Translation: Japan

Creative Project

Made by Antony Vasiliev 1820554


How to Play

Move with 'w', 's', 'a', 'd' to move 'up', 'down', 'left', 'right'

Collect food (yellow) scattered around the area and bring it to your village before the day ends (bar on top)

If you do not bring enough food back within the time limit the village starves


New food appears every 5 days meaning you have to venture further and further as days pass until new food appears


There are soldiers (green) roaming around the area and will chase you if they see you outside the village

If they catch you you are sent back to the village and all the food you were carrying on you is taken away




Note

Walls are randomly generated, if they generate weirdly, refresh the page

Depending on your display resolution, some parts may not draw properly

Designed for 1920x1080 displays, and the use of a keyboard and mouse

Should work in most cases however
